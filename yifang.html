about store

since 2016, yifang fruit tea has been known for its authentic Taiwanese boba tea, using the freshest ingredients. aside from their classic brown sugar drinks, they also offer a wide selection of fruit teas made with locally sourced ingredients (costco). yifang has several locations across the bay area, with three in SF.

天仁茗茶

about store

tenren's tea speaks for itself. in 1953, they started out as a company selling tea leaves and ginseng products, and soon became one of the largest tea shops in Asia. while their drink menu is pretty simple, each drink is handcrafted using their the most flavorful teas. their focus on quality never takes away from their quality tea leaves.

my review

what i like about tenren is the simplicity of their drink menu. they really focus on the quality of their tea. they sell pu'er milktea, something i haven't seen in any other shop. i love a black milk tea, and their tea is always brewed right.
